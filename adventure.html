Adventure Escapes

Thrilling journeys for the bold-hearted

Queenstown, New Zealand

From skydiving to bungee jumping, Queenstown is the adrenaline capital for thrill-seekers.

Also enjoy whitewater rafting, mountain biking, and snow sports in New Zealand's scenic playground.

Interlaken, Switzerland

Experience canyoning, paragliding, and alpine hiking in this Swiss adventure paradise.

Interlaken is also a base for ice climbing and glacier trekking for winter thrill lovers.

Patagonia, Argentina

Explore rugged mountains, glaciers, and remote treks in this breathtaking wilderness.

Hike Torres del Paine or kayak near icebergs for unforgettable nature-based adventures.

Moab, USA

Home to Arches and Canyonlands, Moab is perfect for rock climbing, mountain biking, and hiking.

Jeep tours, BASE jumping, and desert stargazing are also favorite extreme experiences here.

Costa Rica

Zipline through rainforests, surf Pacific waves, and discover active volcanoes on this adventure-packed escape.

Don’t miss river rafting in Turrialba or night hiking in Monteverde Cloud Forest.

Leh-Ladakh, India

Ride through the world’s highest motorable roads and camp under the stars in Himalayan valleys.

Ladakh also offers frozen river trekking and Buddhist monastery trails for spiritual adventurers.
