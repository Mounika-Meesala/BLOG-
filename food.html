Wholesome Food Ideas

Delicious recipes for a balanced lifestyle

Avocado Toast

Healthy, quick, and tasty — top your toast with mashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a sprinkle of chili flakes.

Try adding poached eggs or hemp seeds for added protein!

Overnight Oats

Mix oats, milk, and honey overnight. Add fruits and nuts in the morning for a fiber-rich breakfast.

Chia seeds and cinnamon enhance both taste and health benefits.

Buddha Bowl

Colorful veggies, quinoa, and hummus — a nourishing bowl packed with vitamins and protein.

Drizzle tahini-lemon dressing for an extra zing.

Fruit Smoothie

Blend seasonal fruits with yogurt or almond milk. Refreshing and perfect for post-workout recovery.

Add flax seeds or protein powder for an energy boost.

Chickpea Salad

A protein-rich salad with chickpeas, cucumber, onion, lemon juice, and olive oil.

Add feta cheese and mint leaves for Mediterranean flair.

Grilled Veggies

Seasonal vegetables grilled to perfection — a low-calorie, high-flavor meal with herbs and olive oil.

Best paired with whole grain bread or brown rice.
