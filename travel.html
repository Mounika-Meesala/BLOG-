Budget-Friendly Gateways

Travel smart without breaking the bank

Hanoi, Vietnam

Enjoy delicious street food, charming Old Quarters, and budget hostels in one of Asia’s most affordable capitals.

Lisbon, Portugal

From scenic viewpoints to pastel de nata, Lisbon offers a vibrant European experience at low cost.

Kraków, Poland

With medieval architecture and hearty cuisine, Kraków is perfect for travelers seeking history on a budget.

Bali, Indonesia

Experience lush rice terraces, temples, and beaches—all while staying in beautiful yet affordable homestays.

Mexico City, Mexico

Feast on tacos, explore museums, and enjoy buzzing culture in one of Latin America's top-value destinations.

Prague, Czech Republic

Known as the “City of a Hundred Spires”, Prague delivers fairytale charm at wallet-friendly prices.
